# How to save Instagram pictures

#### 1. Open Instagram Client, tap right top button on the post you want to save.
![Guide201](/images/Guide201.jpg)

#### 2. Choose "Share to..."
![Guide202](/images/Guide202.jpg)

#### 3. Tap "Add to Dagon" menu item.
![Guide203](/images/Guide203.jpg)

#### 4. Tap "Save" button to save. Remember to turn on "Save All Images".
![Guide204](/images/Guide204.jpg)

#### 5. Open Dagon App, the Instagram saved post will appear in list.
![Guide205](/images/Guide205.jpg)

#### 6. You can tap Gallery button on the navigation bar next to New button to browse the pictures you just saved before.
![Guide206](/images/Guide206.jpg)
