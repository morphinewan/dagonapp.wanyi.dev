# How to share URL in Safari

#### 1. Open Safari and Tap the Action Button.
![Guide001](/images/Guide001.jpg)

#### 2. You will see the iOS Acton Sheet.
![Guide002](/images/Guide002.jpg)

#### 3. You will see "Add to Dagon" menu item appearing here. Then tap "Edit Actions".
![Guide003](/images/Guide003.jpg)

#### 4. You can re-arrange the item order, move Dagon menu item to top if you want.
![Guide004](/images/Guide004.jpg)

#### 5. Tap "Add to Dagon" menu item.
![Guide005](/images/Guide005.jpg)

#### 6. Tap "Save" button.
![Guide006](/images/Guide006.jpg)
